Our Drone
Features

Seed Detection
Technology

ReGrowth drones have been fitted with
the worlds first smart micro-processing chip
that communicates with the internal systems
of the drone to detect the type of seed it
is given to plant, before providing a
blueprint of the best locations and distances
the seeds should be planted at.

Automatic
Optimization

The ReGrowth drone collects data from
weather records and sunlight exposures
to provide information to the planter
on where the best locations to plant
seeds are and how that will allow the
plants to absorb maximum sunlight and
nutrients.

New & Improved Camera

8k Camera
360° View
12x Optical Zoom
Built In Land Scanner

Most Powerful And Efficient
Motor Yet...

With a super powerful and durable motor the Regrowth drone is capable of flying
through many different conditions with a flight time over 7 hours per charge. We have designed
the motor to be light weight and compact yet smart in conserving power and energy with a semi-solar powered
component allowing 2 of the 4 propellers to run off solar power, ultimately enabling a longer lasting battery life
