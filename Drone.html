Most Powerful And Efficient
Motor Yet...

With a super powerful and durable motor the Regrowth drone is capable of flying
through many different conditions with a flight time over 7 hours per charge. We have designed

component allowing 2 of the 4 propellers to run off solar power, ultimately enabling a longer lasting battery life.
